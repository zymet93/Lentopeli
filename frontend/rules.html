Guide

Game rules

Player starts with 24 time limit and 1000$. The objective is to get as much money as you can in the time limit
1 Time unit is used everytime the player flies or works in a country
Flight costs are calculated by distance 1km=1$
If you run out of money or get robbed you lose the game.

Profession

At the start of the game the player chooses a profession.
Profession affects the amount of pay when working on
your own field of work. If your profession matches the work type you get double salary

Work

Every country enables the player to work once. And every country randomises to give you 1-3 options of the working types in the game.
Everytime you work you lose 1 time unit

Goals

Goals are randomised upon creating the game. There are 30 countries to fly and random encounters are listed here:
2 Airports you will get robbed and you lose the game
3 Airports have 1000$ prize
2 Airports have 3000$ prize
1 Airport has 50 000$ prize
